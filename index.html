Documentary Filmmaking Workshop

Documenting Sustainability and Happiness in Finland

Workshop Overview

Dates: Nov 11–16, 2025

Location: To be confirmed

Tutor: Martin Fazeli – Director & Filmmaker

This hands-on 6-day workshop guides emerging filmmakers to create short documentaries (5–10 minutes) exploring sustainability, welfare, and happiness in Finland.

Themes & Objectives

  • Ethical documentary storytelling & theory
  • Technical skills: camera, lighting, sound, editing
  • Exploring sustainability and happiness
  • Team-based creative collaboration

Workshop Schedule

  1. Day 1: Introduction, screening & brainstorming
  2. Day 2: Narrative structures, interviews, gear
  3. Day 3: Interviews + mentoring
  4. Day 4: B-roll shooting & rough editing
  5. Day 5: Editing + group feedback
  6. Day 6: Final editing & prep
  7. Day 7: Screening & discussion

Director Bio

Hossein Martin Fazeli is an award-winning filmmaker with over 20 years of experience in narrative and documentary production. His work has aired on BBC, CBC, ARTE and more, and has received 45+ international awards. He has collaborated with UNDP, the EU, and lectured worldwide.
